Our story began in a small Chinatown Library in the depths of Manhattan a few months ago...
Eventually, through rigorous brewing courses and training in culinary small plates and no sleep, we combined our expertise to form 'Brew n Bites.' With one united vision in mind, we wanted to craft the cleanest, crispiest beer that masterfully balance one's thirst and appetite! Our mission was and continues to be to craft beers with lasting complex flavors and fresh food that you can enjoy with us or at home.
